Sales Application Dua Putri: Efficient Sales Management

In this project, I developed a sales application tailored for 'Warung Nasi Bungkus' (wrapped rice stalls), featuring roles for superadmin, admin, and cashier. The dashboard manages products, customers, orders, and staff via CRUD operations while tracking daily earnings by payment methods like cash, QRIS, and transfers. It also includes a dark mode and light mode toggle for user convenience.
