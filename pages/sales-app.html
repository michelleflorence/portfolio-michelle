Sales Application Dua Putri - Empowering Efficient Sales Management

In this project, I developed a sales application specifically for 'Warung Nasi Bungkus' (wrapped rice stalls) with three primary roles: superadmin, admin, and cashier. The application features a dashboard focused on managing products, customers, orders, and staff through CRUD operations. The dashboard is also capable of recording daily earnings from the sales application, with income data available based on payment methods such as cash, QRIS, and transfers. Not only that, this dashboard also includes a feature that allows users to switch between dark mode and light mode.
